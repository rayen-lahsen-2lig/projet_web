Always remember that responsible adoption changes the life of the animal as much as yours

To adopt a pet is not an easy decision.
Many people afraid of to adopt a pet because they do not want to take care of them.
In my opinion, it looks like a kind of egoism from their side because every animal in a shelter is waiting for love and care from human side.
Here exist many shelters where different kinds of animals live and wait for their future owners.
Any person can make the life of these animals better showing the own mercy to homeless animals through their adoption.

What are our conditions for adopting a pet?

  • From an administrative point of view, the adoption of an animal is reserved for adults.
  • You will also have some supporting documents to provide to formalize the adoption:
  • An ID card ;
  • Proof of address less than 3 months old;
  • Proof of income to adopt a dog.
  • An identity photo.
