We aim to make LOST AND SEEN the central lost and found database

Here are some easy ways to help lost pets in your area right now.


Spread the word online

Take a look at our local lost and seen instagram page,
which is a great ways to tap into what’s happening in your community.
Visit the lost and seen database and view lost and found pets in your area.
If you’ve found a missing dog or cat, snap a picture and upload it to the site.

Hand out flyers in your neighborhood

Download and print lost and found pet flyers from lost and seen
and distribute them in veterinary offices, coffee shops,
grocery stores, and other local businesses.

Organize a lost pet search

Work with your local animal shelter to coordinate a community search.
Direct volunteers to look carefully in the neighborhood, around houses, neighbors’ yards, parks,
and other places where a dog or cat might be inclined to hide.


Let your voice be heard: sign a petition to help animals in need!

Let's work together to support the cause against animal cruelty
